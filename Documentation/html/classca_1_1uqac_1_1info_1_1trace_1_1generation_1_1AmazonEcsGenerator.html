Detailed Description

Generate a random trace of Amazon ECS shopping cart requests and responses. The constraints followed by the sequence of messages are detailed in: <blockquote> S. Hallé, R. Villemaire. (2012). Constraint-Based Invocation of Stateful Web Services: the Beep Store (Case Study). In <cite>4th International Workshop on Principles of Engineering Service-Oriented Systems (PESOS 2012)</cite>. </blockquote>

Author:
Sylvain Hallé
