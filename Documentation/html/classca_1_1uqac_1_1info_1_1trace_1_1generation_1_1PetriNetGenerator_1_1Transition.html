Detailed Description

A transition in a Petri net is a graph node that is linked via incoming and outgoing arrows to places. A transition of a Petri net may fire whenever there are sufficient tokens in the places from all incoming arcs; when it fires, it consumes these tokens, and places tokens at the end of all output arcs.
